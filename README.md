## Who I Am

Joined Bootalk as **Chief Data Officer (CDO)**, rapidly expanded scope to lead the entire engineering organization. After the CTO's departure in August 2025, took on **Technical Lead / Product Owner** responsibilities — managing a 4-person dev team, owning all platform deployments, and driving product decisions across web, mobile, backend, and data.

Also served as **Privacy Protection Officer (개인정보처리담당자)**, handled partner integrations (Nonghyup, Lotte Card, Daelim Bath), co-founded AI subsidiary **BOOAI (부아이)**, and contributed to business operations including investor relations, regulatory compliance, and AI product development (SemuGPT, 부토기).
